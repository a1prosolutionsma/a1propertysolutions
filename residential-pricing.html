Pricing That Matches Real Home Maintenance

Most residential jobs in Boston, Brookline, Cambridge, Somerville, Newton, and nearby towns fall into one of three buckets: a one-time repair visit, seasonal upkeep, or recurring maintenance. We quote each one clearly so you know what is included before we schedule the work.

How residential pricing works: Most home service appointments are completed as part of a service visit. Residential service visits start at $150 and include up to ~2 hours of work. Additional time is billed at $95/hour. The task prices shown below are common starting points for work completed during a service visit or maintenance plan.
Many of the small interior services listed on this page may also be a fit for renters and tenants, including mounting, assembly, punch-list work, and other approved non-structural jobs. Tenants should confirm approval for any work that requires landlord or property-management authorization.
