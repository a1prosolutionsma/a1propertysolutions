Common Questions

HomeShield FAQs

Does this plan include repairs? ▾
No. HomeShield Basic is for routine maintenance tasks only. If something needs repair, we can point it out and quote the work separately at your member discount rate.
Are materials like filters and batteries included? ▾
Not in the plan price. Filters, batteries, and bulbs can either be customer-supplied or billed separately at cost with your approval.
What if I need more than what the Basic plan covers? ▾
Additional services like repairs, gutter cleaning, pressure washing, and other project work can all be quoted separately. HomeShield members receive 5% off add-on services.
Is there a contract or automatic renewal? ▾
No contracts and no automatic billing without approval. You pay once for the year, and we can check in before renewal if you want to continue.
When do the visits happen? ▾
Usually one visit in spring and one in fall. We reach out ahead of time to help schedule both visits.
Is this available for condos, rentals, or multi-family properties? ▾
In many cases, yes. The exact scope depends on the property type, layout, and access. Reach out and we’ll let you know whether HomeShield Basic is a good fit.
