Common Questions

Pricing FAQs

Do you charge for estimates? ▾
No — estimates are always free. Call or text with your project details and we’ll give you a clear quote before any work begins.
What’s your minimum service call? ▾
Our minimum service call is $150. For hourly handyman work, there is also a 2-hour minimum unless the job is quoted at a flat rate.
Are materials included in the prices? ▾
Most prices are for labor only unless otherwise noted. Materials are billed separately at cost and approved with you upfront.
Do you require a deposit? ▾
For larger jobs, special-order materials, or material-heavy projects, we may require a deposit before scheduling the work.
How does the A1 HomeShield Plan work? ▾
You pay once per year and get two seasonal visits. The plan is built around routine upkeep items, simple check-ins, and easier scheduling for homeowners who want regular maintenance support without managing every reminder themselves.
Can I bundle cleanup services? ▾
Absolutely. Bundling cleanup, gutters, trimming, and light washing into one visit usually saves both time and money.
What if the job takes longer than expected? ▾
If a job runs beyond the approved scope, we stop and communicate with you before doing any additional billable work. No surprise invoices.
What forms of payment do you accept? ▾
We accept cash, check, Venmo, Zelle, and major credit cards. Payment is due upon job completion unless you’re on a recurring maintenance plan.
