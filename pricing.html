Common Questions

Pricing FAQs

Do you charge for estimates? ▾
No — estimates are always free. Call or text us with your project details and we'll give you a clear quote before any work begins.
What's the minimum for a handyman call? ▾
There's a 2-hour minimum for hourly handyman work. For flat-rate jobs (like furniture assembly or a door repair), we quote the job directly — no hourly minimum applies.
Are materials included in the pricing? ▾
Most prices listed are for labor only. Materials are billed separately at cost. We'll always tell you upfront what materials are needed and what they'll cost.
Do you offer discounts for recurring service? ▾
Yes — our monthly maintenance packages offer significant savings compared to individual service calls. See the packages above, or call us to discuss a custom arrangement.
What forms of payment do you accept? ▾
We accept cash, check, Venmo, Zelle, and major credit cards. Payment is due upon job completion unless you're on a monthly package.
What if the job takes longer than expected? ▾
If a job is going to run over the quoted time or scope, we'll always communicate with you before doing additional work. No surprise bills — ever.
