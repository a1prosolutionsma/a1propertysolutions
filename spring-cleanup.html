Spring Cleanup FAQs

Questions Homeowners Ask Before Booking

Are these prices the same as the yardwork pricing on the residential page?▾
Yes. This page follows the same exterior and seasonal cleanup rates shown on the residential pricing page: leaf and debris cleanup from $125, full yard cleanup from $250, gutter cleaning from $175, shrub or hedge trimming from $95, and light pressure washing from $150.
What makes the price go up?▾
Larger yards, heavy debris, difficult access, steeper gutters, overgrown shrubs, extra hauling, and multiple bundled tasks can all affect final pricing. We quote the scope clearly before scheduling.
Can I combine cleanup with gutters or trimming?▾
Absolutely. That is one of the most common spring requests. Bundling cleanup, gutters, trimming, and light washing into one visit usually makes the service more efficient.
Do you handle small and large properties?▾
Yes. Some jobs are quick resets for smaller yards and others are fuller seasonal cleanups. The scope is matched to the size and condition of the property.
