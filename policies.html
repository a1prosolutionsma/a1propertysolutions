Before You Book

Pricing & Policies Made Clear Up Front

This page explains how estimates, scheduling, deposits, materials, payment, and scope approvals work so you know what to expect before your appointment is booked.

Photo-Based Estimates $150 Minimum Service Call Deposits May Be Required
Why we do this: These policies help us provide reliable service, protect reserved time, and keep quoting and scheduling straightforward for every client.
What to Expect

Booking Expectations Without Surprises

Most jobs can be quoted from photos and a clear description of the work. When a project needs an on-site walkthrough before pricing, we will let you know that up front. Appointments are confirmed upon estimate approval or deposit.

📷 Estimate Policy

Free estimates are available for most jobs based on photos and project details. If an in-person quote visit is needed, a $50 site visit fee may apply and will be credited toward the job.

🚐 In-Person Site Visits

When a property needs to be seen in person before pricing, the visit will be treated as a quote visit or scope review. If a site visit fee applies, it is credited toward the approved job.

💼 Minimum Service Call

Minimum service call is $150.

📅 Deposits & Scheduling

Deposits may be required for larger jobs, material-heavy projects, special-order materials, or reserved time blocks. Appointments are confirmed upon estimate approval or deposit.

💳 Payment Methods

Accepted payment methods are cash, check, Zelle, Venmo, and major credit/debit cards.

➕ Credit Card Fee

Card payments include a 3% processing fee.

🧾 Check Policy

Checks must clear. Do not accept third-party or overpayment checks.

⏱️ Payment Timing

Unless otherwise agreed in writing, payment is due upon completion.

🛠️ Added Work Approval

Any additional work outside the approved scope must be discussed and approved before continuing.

🔁 Cancellation / Rescheduling

Please provide as much notice as possible if rescheduling. Missed appointments or late cancellations may result in a service charge when significant time has already been reserved.

📦 Materials / Reimbursement

Customer-supplied materials are allowed. If materials are sourced by A1 Property Solutions, those costs must be approved and billed separately.

🧰 Workmanship Concerns

Any workmanship concerns should be reported within 7 calendar days of job completion so they can be reviewed promptly and in good faith.

Call, text, or send your project details and we will tell you the fastest next step for pricing and booking.
