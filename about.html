About A1

Built on Hard Work & Reliability

A locally owned property maintenance company serving Greater Boston with dependable service, clear communication, and professional results.

Meet the Owner
John Liriano
Owner & Operator

Hi, I’m the owner of A1 Property Pros, providing reliable property maintenance services throughout the Boston area for homeowners, landlords, property managers, and commercial clients.

I built this business around one core principle: consistency. Property owners should not have to chase contractors, deal with poor communication, or question whether the work will be done properly. My focus is on delivering dependable service, clear expectations, and work that meets a professional standard.

I handle everything from yard cleanups and exterior maintenance to repairs and ongoing property upkeep. Whether the job is at a residential home or a commercial property, the approach stays the same — show up on time, communicate clearly, and take care of the property the right way.

A1 Property Pros is built to be a reliable long-term resource, not just a one-time service call. If you’re looking for someone who takes ownership of the work and values doing the job right, you’re in the right place.
