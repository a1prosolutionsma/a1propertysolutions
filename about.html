Our Story

Meet the Owner
John Liriano
Owner & Operator

Hi, I’m the owner of A1 Property Solutions, providing reliable property maintenance services throughout the Boston area for homeowners, landlords, and commercial clients.

I built this business around one core principle — consistency. Property owners shouldn’t have to chase contractors, deal with poor communication, or question the quality of work being done. My focus is on delivering dependable service, clear expectations, and results that meet a professional standard.

I handle everything from yard cleanups and exterior maintenance to repairs and ongoing property upkeep. Whether I’m working on a residential property or supporting a commercial client, the approach stays the same — show up on time, do the work right, and make sure the property is taken care of properly.

A1 Property Solutions is designed to be a long-term, reliable partner, not just a one-time service.

If you’re looking for someone who takes ownership of the work and delivers consistently, you’re in the right place.
