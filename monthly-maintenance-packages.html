Common Questions

Monthly Package FAQs

Are these flat-rate contracts for every possible repair?▾
No. The package gives you recurring maintenance visits and a clearer ongoing scope, but larger repairs, material-heavy work, or special projects are still quoted separately before anything extra is done.
Can the package be customized for my property?▾
Yes. That is especially common for multifamily, mixed-use, and commercial properties where access, building size, and documentation needs vary from site to site.
Is HomeShield the same thing as a monthly package?▾
No. HomeShield Basic is an annual homeowner plan with two seasonal visits. Monthly maintenance packages are for people who want more regular recurring service throughout the year.
What if I only need recurring service for part of the year?▾
That can still work. Some clients need heavier support during turnover seasons, spring cleanup, or peak operating months. We can talk through what schedule makes sense.
